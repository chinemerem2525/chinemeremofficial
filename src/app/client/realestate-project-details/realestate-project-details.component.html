Overview

The Real Estate Platform is crafted to deliver a sleek, intuitive, and powerful digital experience for users seeking to buy, rent, or sell property. Blending elegant design with robust functionality, it offers comprehensive features such as interactive dashboards, dynamic property listings, integrated payment solutions, and real-time notifications. Optimized for speed and responsiveness, this platform serves as a premium, conversion-focused hub for real estate transactions. By streamlining workflows and enhancing engagement, it empowers agents, property owners, and prospective buyers or tenants to achieve their goals with ease and confidence.

That is where I come in. To develop interactive dashboards, property listings, user profiles, and search functionality to ensure a seamless user experience. Then I will ensure the platform is fast, responsive, and scalable across devices and browsers.

  • Stack: Angular, Node.Js, Express.Js & MongoDB
  • Tools: VS Code, Postman, MongoDB Compass
